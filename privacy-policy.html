Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2025

Tazematik (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Tazematik mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous Usage: You do not need to create an account to use Tazematik. All users are authenticated anonymously via Firebase.
  • Device Information: We collect a unique device identifier (device ID) to manage your free analysis quota and prevent abuse.
  • Photos: When you analyze a fruit or vegetable, the photo you select is uploaded to our secure cloud storage for processing. Photos are not used for any other purpose and are deleted after analysis.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (e.g., which features are used most).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-based produce analysis and manage your analysis quota.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To prevent abuse and ensure fair use of free analysis rights.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.
  • We may use third-party analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics) for anonymous usage statistics.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including secure cloud storage and encrypted connections.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Photos are deleted after analysis is complete.
  • You can request deletion of your device data by contacting us at support@tazematik.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tazematik is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at support@tazematik.com.
