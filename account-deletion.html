Account / Data Deletion Request

Tazematik does not require you to create an account. All users are authenticated anonymously and no personal account information is stored.

Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete all data associated with your device (including analysis history and quota), please send an email to support@tazematik.com with the subject “Account or Data Deletion Request”.

We will process your request and delete all device-related data from our systems within 7 days.
